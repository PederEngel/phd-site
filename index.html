Tele-Ultrasound with Mixed Reality (eFAST)

PhD project — Peder C. Engelsen · Oslo University Hospital & University of Oslo

About

We study telementored eFAST to help non-experts detect pneumothorax and hemoperitoneum. Study I showed feasibility during helicopter flight; Study II evaluates diagnostic accuracy for hemoperitoneum after liver surgery (NCT06594562).
